Privacy Policy

At Age of Sosaria, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our players and community members.

Information We Collect

  • We may collect personal information when you:
  • • Create or use an account on the server
  • • Interact with community features or support systems
  • • Contact staff for assistance
  • • Make voluntary donations
  • This information may include:
  • • Email address
  • • Account or character information
  • • IP address and connection data

How We Use Information

  • • Operate and maintain the server
  • • Provide account services and support
  • • Improve gameplay and stability
  • • Communicate important updates
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information. Limited data may be shared with trusted service providers only when necessary.
  • • To operate and maintain your game account
  • • To detect and prevent cheating or rule violations
  • • To respond to support requests and disputes
  • • To send important shard updates and announcements
  • • We never sell or share your data with third parties

Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee complete security over the internet.

Rule Enforcement

In cases involving rule violations, we may retain relevant information for investigations or dispute resolution.

Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws such as GDPR or CCPA, including access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Continued use of the server indicates acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact staff through official support channels.
